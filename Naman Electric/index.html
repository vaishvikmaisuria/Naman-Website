Services

Naman Electric is proud to offer a wide range of electrical services. Including, but not limited to the following

Interior and exterior lighting

Interior lights including pot lights, kitchen cabinet lights, fixtures, valance lighting, high bay light, chandeliers, dimmer-switches, and decors-receptacle upgrade. Exterior lights including pot lights, patio lights and more.

Electrical Panels

We can rewire, replace, or install new electrical panels with ESA safety requirements. We also upgrade panel fuses to breakers, support service mast install, and emergency repairs.

Aluminum Wiring

We provide wire reconditioning, aluminum/copper wiring pigtailing, and we will help you get ESA certification for insurance claims.

Renovations

We can also help with new basement, kitchen, bathroom, hot tub, kitchen range hoods, and power equipment wiring/installation.

Infrared Thermal Inspections

we scan electrical cabinets, all types of electrical panel, bolted connections, transformers, motors, and switch gear for infrared thermal inspection.

Other

We troubleshoot and fix power outages, short circuits, surge protectors, GFCI receptacles and AFCI circuits. We also do exit/emergency lighting, smoke/carbon monoxide detector, and ballast replacement.
